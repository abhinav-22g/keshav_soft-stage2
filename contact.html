Lower impedance (16Ω) headphones are easier to drive and work well with phones and portable devices. Higher impedance (32Ω+) headphones need more power but often deliver better sound quality with proper amplification.

FLAC is lossless compression, preserving every detail of the original recording. MP3 removes data to reduce file size. The difference is most noticeable on high-quality systems and with trained ears, but both can sound great depending on your setup and preferences.
